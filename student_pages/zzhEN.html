Zehua Zang (臧泽华)


Postdoctoral Researcher

Supervisor: Prof. Jiahuan Zhou

Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University.

Beijing, China

E-mail: zehua2020@iscas.ac.cn


I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University. I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences in June 2026. Before that, I received my B.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University in June 2019. My research interests include reinforcement learning and continual learning for embodied intelligence.
